# AI for Social Impact: Learning and Planning in the Data-to-Deployment Pipeline

## Response

The research on AI for Social Impact faces several limitations. One major limitation is the scarcity of data in social impact settings, which necessitates strategies for data acquisition and handling sparse data. Additionally, the research requires interdisciplinary partnerships to address complex social problems effectively. Another limitation is the challenge of ensuring fairness in AI systems, as questions of bias and fairness are complex and require further exploration. Finally, the evaluation of AI for Social Impact work differs from traditional AI research, requiring significant time and effort in developing partnerships and conducting field studies to achieve concrete impacts.
