# Experimental Search for Quantum Gravity

## Question

Write a 2 sentence summary as a MD file download.

## Answer

The paper "Experimental Search for Quantum Gravity" by Sabine Hossenfelder provides a survey of current and planned experimental tests for quantum gravity, discussing phenomenological models and experimental areas where these models can be tested or constrained. It highlights the challenges faced in the field, such as the similarity of predictions from different models and the need for collaboration between theorists and experimentalists.
