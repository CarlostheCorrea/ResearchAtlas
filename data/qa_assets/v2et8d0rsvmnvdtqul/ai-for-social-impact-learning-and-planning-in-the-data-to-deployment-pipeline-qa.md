# AI for Social Impact: Learning and Planning in the Data-to-Deployment Pipeline

## Response

The Data-to-Deployment Pipeline is a solution approach used in AI for Social Impact research. It involves several stages: immersion, where researchers gather data and understand the problem domain; predictive modeling, where models are built to predict high-risk cases; prescriptive algorithm development, where game-theoretic or multiagent systems reasoning is used to optimize objectives; and field tests and deployment, where solutions are tested and validated in real-world settings. This pipeline is designed to ensure that AI research leads to tangible social impact by directly addressing societal problems through a structured process from data collection to deployment in the field.
